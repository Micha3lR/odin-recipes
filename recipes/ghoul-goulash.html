Ghoul Goulash

A tantalizing dish from across the veil!

Ghoul Goulash is a hauntingly delicious dish that pleases the taste buds with its rich, savory flavors. Each spoonful is a delightful dance of tender, slow-cooked meat that falls apart at the slightest touch, mingling with a symphony of spices that evoke the essence of autumn’s chill. The earthy tones of fresh herbs and the subtle heat of paprika give this goulash its signature warmth, while the addition of plump, juicy worms offers a playful twist on texture, ensuring a culinary experience like no other.

Whether you’re a ghoul, ghost, or simply a lover of fine food, this goulash promises an otherworldly delight that will leave you craving more.
