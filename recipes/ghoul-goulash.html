Ghoul Goulash

A tantalizing dish from across the veil!

Ghoul Goulash is a hauntingly delicious dish that pleases the taste buds with its rich, savory flavors. Each spoonful is a delightful dance of tender, slow-cooked meat that falls apart at the slightest touch, mingling with a symphony of spices that evoke the essence of autumn’s chill. The earthy tones of fresh herbs and the subtle heat of paprika give this goulash its signature warmth, while the addition of plump, juicy worms offers a playful twist on texture, ensuring a culinary experience like no other.

Whether you’re a ghoul, ghost, or simply a lover of fine food, this goulash promises an otherworldly delight that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

Sinister Sauce
  • 1 cauldron of crushed tomatoes, cursed by the night
  • A dollop of tomato paste, as dark as the abyss
Ghastly Garnish
  • Sour scream, to echo through the haunted halls
  • Sliced olives, plucked from the eyes of forgotten statues
Vegetables from the Vile Void
  • 1 onion, chopped with a silver blade
  • 1 green pepper, harvested at the witching hour
  • Cloves of garlic, to ward of the lurking shadows
  • Frozen corn, the last remnants of a cursed harvest
Meat from the Macabre
  • Ground beef or braised banshee steak
Seasoning of the Shadows
  • Salt, harvested from the tears of the forlorn
  • Black pepper, ground from the charred remains of a phoenix
  • Red pepper flakes, to summon the fire demons
  • Fresh basil, plucked from the garden of the grim reaper
  • Paprika, the dust of ancient, crumbling tombs
  • Smoked paprika, infused with the essence of spectral flames
Serving Suggestions
  • Baked potatoes, hollowed out like the skulls of the vanquished
  • Cooked pasta, the tangled webs of fate
Extras
  • Plump, juicy worms

Steps

  1. Summon the Spirits

    • Begin by conjuring the spirits of the kitchen and lighting the experience candlelight to set the mood for your ghastly culinary adventure.

  2. Prepare the Sinister Sauce

    • In a cauldron over medium heat, sauté the chopped onion with a silver blade until it whispers secrets of the underworld.
    • Add the green pepper harvested at the witching hour and let them conspire together until tender.

  3. Awaken the Meat from the Macabre

    • Crumble the ground beast into the cauldron and cook until it's brown as the earth from which it came.
    • Banish any excess spirits (fat) that may have surfaced.

  4. Cast the Seasoning Spell

    • Sprinkle the salt harvested from the tears of the forlorn, black pepper ground from the charred remains of a pheonix, and other shadowy seasonings.
    • Stir well to enchant every morsel

  5. Unleash the Ghastly Garnish

    • Add the crushed tomatoes, cursed by the night, and the dark abyss of tomato paste.
    • Stir well and let the mixture bubble like a witch's brew.

  6. Invoke the Vegetables from the Vile Void

    • Toss in the frozen corn, the last remnants of a cursed harvest, and let the mixture simmer until the vegetables are infused with the essence of spectral flames.

  7. Ensnare the Worms

    • Gently fold in the plump, juicy worms, ensuring they twist and turn through the goulash, adding a playful scare with every bite.

  8. Serve with a Whisper of the Damned

    • Ladle the ghoul goulash into the hollowed-out baked potatoes or over the tangled webs of cooked pasta.
    • Garnish with sour scream and sliced olives plucked from the eyes of forgotten statues.

  9. Reveal the Ancient Cryptic Symbol

    • As you serve, unveil the ancient, cryptic symbol etched onto the plate, inviting your guests to decipher its mysteries as they dine.

  10. Final Incantation

    • With the shadowy figure watching over you, present the dish under the flickering candlelight.
    • Recite an incantation to bless the meal with flavors that will haunt the palate for eternity.
